Hannah's Past

I was born December 10, 1999 in Peoria, Illinois into a family with an 2 1/2 year old sister, an orange cat, and two parents. I was heavy baby, weighing 9.12 lbs. After about one and a half years, we moved to Birmingham, Alabama and lived there for about five years. I moved to Greenville, South Carolina when I was six and attended Oakview Elementary from first to fifth grade. Starting in sixth grade, I went to Beck Academy through eighth grade. During my elementary and middle school career, I praticipated in activities like dance, gymnastics, soccer, and chorus.

My Present

Currently, I am a junior in highschool at J.L. Mann. My parents are 54 and 56 and my sister is 19. My dad is a professor at Furman University and teaches international business and marketing. My sister is a sophomore at Furman University. I dance about five days a week and I am currently rehearsing for "Ghosts" and "The Nutcracker". In school, I take difficult classes and participate in school clubs like Mann Nation, French Club, and NHS.

I work at Publix in downtown Greenville. I just put in my two week notice to resign because I have trouble balancing all of my responsibilities. I am a bagger and cashier.

My Future

In my future, I plan to attend Furman University for four years. I have not decided what I would like to major in yet but I want to study abroad somewhere in Europe. I will hopefully have a successful career and travel everywhere in my twenties. I will marry someone I love and have a golden doodle puppy and two kids. My family and I will travel frequently to the mountains and to foreign lands. This will all be possible if I get my act together and start getting serious about my grades.
